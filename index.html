A Letter to You

Dearest Mama,

There are no words that are big enough to hold everything I feel for you. You have been my safe place, my home, and my greatest teacher — not through lessons you sat me down to give, but through the quiet way you lived your life with grace, patience, and so much love.

I have watched you hold our family together through storms I didn't even know were happening. You carried so much in silence, so that the rest of us could carry so little. That kind of love is rare, and I am so lucky it has always been mine.

Thank you for every prayer you said for me. Thank you for every meal you made with your hands. Thank you for every time you looked at me like I was the most important person in the room — because that look is part of what made me who I am today.

I love you more than I will ever be able to say. But I will spend my whole life trying to show you.

Forever yours ❤️
